About Museo Bulawan

Museo Bulawan

Known as the “Golden Museum,” Museo Bulawan is the leading and most viewer-friendly community museum that serves as the nerve center for education and communication of the rich cultural, artistic, and natural heritage and history of Camarines Norte. It is the ultimate medium for preservation and exhibition of significant objects that strengthen the values of the people and deepen their patriotism and sense of identity.
